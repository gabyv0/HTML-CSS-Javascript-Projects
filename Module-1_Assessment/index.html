About Me

My name is Gaby, short for Gabriela. I graduated from DePaul University in Chicago in June of 2019 with a Bachelor of Science in Mathematics and Computer Science, where the majority of the focus was on mathematics.

Besides my interest in Math and CS, I like video games, needle work, and film. I mostly do things like cross stitch and embroidery.

I like to think I am pretty good at cross stitch, but my embroidery skills are lacking. Some of my favorite games are Bloodborne, The Legend of Zelda: Twilight Princess, and Persona 4 Golden.

My favorite movies ever are Eraserhead, Mulholland Drive, Burning, and I, Daniel Blake. My two favorite directors are David Lynch and Ken Loach. My boyfriend and I collect Criterion Collection films. It's fun to buy them without watching them before.
